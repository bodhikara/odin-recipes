Burger Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • .5 cup ketchup
  • .25 cup prepared yellow mustard
  • 2 teaspoons dried minced onion
  • .25 teaspoon dried minced garlic
  • .125 teaspoon white vinegar
  • hot sauce to taste
  • seasoned pepper to taste
  • salt to taste

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Whisk together mayonnaise, ketchup, mustard, onion, garlic, and vinegar in a bowl.
  3. Season with hot sauce, salt, and seasoned pepper.
  4. Cover and chill at least 1 hour before serving.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
